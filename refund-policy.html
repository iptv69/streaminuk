2. How to request a refund

Email support@streaminuk.co.uk or message us on WhatsApp with your order details and the reason for your request. Please give our support team a chance to fix any technical issue first — most problems are resolved quickly.

  • Refund requests must be made within the guarantee window for your plan.

  • Refunds are not available for problems caused by your own internet connection, hardware, or incompatible third-party applications.
  • The guarantee does not cover a simple change of mind after extensive use of the service.
