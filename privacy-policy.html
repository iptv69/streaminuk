This Privacy Policy explains how [Company Legal Name] ("StreaminUK", "we", "us") collects, uses and protects your personal data when you visit streaminuk.co.uk or use our service. We process personal data in accordance with the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who we are

StreaminUK is operated by [Company Legal Name], [registered address]. For any privacy questions, contact us at support@streaminuk.co.uk.

2. Information we collect

  • Contact details you give us, such as your name, email address and WhatsApp number.
  • Order information, such as the plan you choose and payment confirmation (we do not store full card details — see section 4).

  • Messages you send us through email, WhatsApp or our contact form.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide your subscription and send your login details.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your enquiries.
  • To process payments and prevent fraud.
  • To improve our website and service.
  • To send service updates and, where you have consented, occasional offers (you can opt out at any time).

4. Payments

Payments are handled by third-party payment providers [payment provider names]. We do not store your full card details on our servers. Your payment data is processed by these providers under their own privacy policies.

5. Lawful bases

We rely on the following lawful bases: performance of a contract (to deliver your subscription), legitimate interests (to run and improve our service securely), legal obligation (record-keeping), and consent (for marketing messages).

6. Sharing your data

We share data only with service providers who help us operate — for example payment processors and email or messaging providers — and only as needed. We do not sell your personal data.

7. Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes above and to meet legal and accounting obligations, after which it is securely deleted.

8. Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict processing of your data, to object to processing, and to data portability. To exercise any of these, email support@streaminuk.co.uk. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

We use essential cookies to run the site and, where you allow it, analytics cookies to understand how the site is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page with the revised date above.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@streaminuk.co.uk.
